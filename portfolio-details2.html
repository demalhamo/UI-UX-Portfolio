Pet Adoption


The Product

Pet Adoption is a simple app for animal shelter which allow users to view, select and adopt the animal. This platform is based on software and database to solve the problem occurs for public who want to adopt or surrender and animal.

User Research



Summary

Before creating it, I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to better understand the needs of the consumers. People those who wants to adopt animal as their pet were the major user groups that were discovered through the research.
I came to the conclusion after evaluating the research that most of people are interested to adopt animal but almost 90% are left out because there are no sources and platform to adopt an animal . If there is platform to adopt animal, then they are willing to adopt animal.

Usability study



Findings

I conducted two rounds of usability studies. Finding from the first study helped guide the designs from wireframes to mockups. The second study used a high-fadelity prototype and revealed what aspects of the mockups needed refining.

Round 1 Findings

1. Users want more customization options

2. Users want a delivery option

Round 2 Finding

1. The checkout process has too many unnecessary steps

2. Got confused between delivery and pickup

Takeaways



Impact:

The idea that i have applied for this project was appreciated by all the reviewers of my project and gave lost of positive comments.

What I learned:

I discovered that developing an app from scratch is not necessary. It is possible to redesign an existing app to make it more accessible to visually impaired users. This will enable the design process to be completed more quickly and affordably, benefiting more people.
